QuizBuzz

Rules

  1. There are Total 5 Questions for each quiz.
  2. For each question you will get 15 seconds to answer.
  3. Once you select the option you cannot change your answer.
  4. After selecting answer you will see a button below options for going to next question.
  5. Once you go to next question you cannot jump to previous question.
  6. After answering all 5 questions you will reach on the result page where you can see your score.
